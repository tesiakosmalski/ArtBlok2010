Historic Research & Development

2010 E Hennepin was originally built by General Mills in 1927. By 1940, 2010 became a campus of 8 different buildings.

The front door led to business offices that planned and marketed products like Bisquick and Cheerios.

The Ryan "Flight Recorder," a.k.a. the The Black Box, and the "Jitterbug" torpedo timer were initially developed at 2010.

Authentic artifacts remain of the tools of science.

Industrial spaces have been converted into platforms for metal working.
